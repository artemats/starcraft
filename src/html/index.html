What is RussianCyber Games

Do you remember the good old World Cyber Games? That tournament shaped e-sport.

Every player wanted to qualify for it and fans worldwide were traveling hundreds of miles to cheer for their favorite players. That’s exactly what RCG is these days. The event is organized by people who love Starcraft for people who love Broodwar.

RCG gives great motivation for players to practice and huge excitement for the viewers to watch. It is the ultimate Championship with the highest prize pool and best players.
Unfortunately, due to COVID-19, it’s impossible to organize an offline event. We will go online for RCG 2020.
